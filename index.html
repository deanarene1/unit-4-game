Crystals Collector!

  • At the beginning of the game, you will be given a random number (shown on right).
  • There are four crystals below. Each crystal has a hidden value that will be added to your total score once clicked.
  • If you perfectly match up your score to the random number, you win! However, if your score goes over the random number amount, you lose!
  • The value of the four crystals changes with each new game start.
